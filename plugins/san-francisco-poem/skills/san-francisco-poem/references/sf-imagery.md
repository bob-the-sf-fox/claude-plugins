# San Francisco imagery

Use these as optional ingredients, not a checklist.

## Atmosphere

- Fog spilling over the hills like a living curtain
- Salt in the air near Ocean Beach
- Bright cold sunlight after a gray morning
- Wind that makes the whole city feel awake
- The strange mix of tenderness and edge

## Visual details

- Cable car sparks and steel tracks on steep streets
- Painted Victorians and bay windows catching late light
- The Golden Gate half-hidden, then suddenly blazing red
- Ferry Building clock tower in the morning
- Chinatown lanterns, North Beach cafes, Mission murals
- Sutro Tower ghosting through fog
- Twin Peaks above a blanket of cloud

## Emotional tones

- Ambition mixed with nostalgia
- Beauty that feels temporary and dramatic
- A city that can be lonely and intimate in the same afternoon
- Reinvention, restlessness, romance, and stubborn local pride

## Useful contrasts

- Tech gloss vs. old neighborhood character
- Pacific wildness vs. compact city streets
- Quiet sunrise coffee vs. noisy night hills
- Grandeur of the bridge vs. small human moments on corners
